Why visit us?

AvaLanche offers wide range of hot, cold and alcoholic drinks, which you can enjoy with a piece of cake or freshly baked croissant. The only place in town to offer a Bubble Tea. An increadibly unique beverage made by mixing a milk or ice cream as a base, flavoured syrup and chewy little spheres that release additional flavour.

What will you find here?

We are located in the heart of the town centre and offer outdoor and indoor seating with a private area for your meetings and a free wi-fi. Family friendly place with a playground in a quite and safe location. Parking available nearby as well.
